

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
cron-io : manage your http://cron.io scheduled jobs.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Usage:

    $ cron-io  your-cronio-username your-cronio-pwd
    $   > get 1
    $   > delete 4
    $   > update 4 name="my job" url="http://example.com" schedule="1 * * * * *"
    $   > create name="my job" url="http://example.com" schedule="1 * * * * *"

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Installation :

    $ gem install cron-io

  Report bugs to http://github.com/alainravet/cron-io
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
